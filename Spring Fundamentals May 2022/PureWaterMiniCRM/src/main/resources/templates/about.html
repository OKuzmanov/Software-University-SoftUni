About

PureWater Mini CRM is a high end software solution for businesses within the water commerce business. Our CRM will help you manage your relationships with all your Suppliers and Customers. It will keep track of all orders allowing you to quickly and easily run ad hoc reports. The dynamic homepage will show you all due orders and it will automatically calculate revenue upon completion. It will also give you insights of all the current users and the count of orders they have created. The CRM will give you up-to-date data for your Product and Raw Materials inventories. Also it will offer user roles solution giving you the optimal control over the permissions of your employees. This application is intended for commercial use only! The subscription plan pricing is the following: - For up to 5 users and 10 orders per user per day the application is free of charge. (Bronze Tier) - For up to 50 users and 150 orders per user per day the monthly fee is $1500. (Silver Tier) - For up to 150 users and 300 orders per user per day the monthly fee is $2500. (Gold Tier) - For unlimited number of users and unlimited orders per user per day the monthly fee is $3000.
